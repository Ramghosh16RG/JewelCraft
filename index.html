Frequently Asked Questions

How long does custom jewelry design take?

The timeline varies depending on the complexity of the design. Typically, custom pieces take 4-6 weeks from consultation to completion.

Can I provide my own gemstones for a custom piece?

Yes, we can incorporate your own gemstones into the design, ensuring they are securely set and beautifully showcased.

Do you offer consultations?

Absolutely! We offer free consultations where you can discuss your ideas with our designers and explore options for your custom jewelry.

What is your return policy?

Custom jewelry is non-refundable, but we strive to ensure your complete satisfaction with the design before finalizing your order.
